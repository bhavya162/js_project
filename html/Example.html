Examples of AI

Voice Assistants –

AI powers virtual assistants like Siri (Apple), Alexa (Amazon), and Google Assistant, which understand voice commands and respond intelligently.

Recommendation Systems –

Platforms like Netflix, YouTube, and Amazon use AI to recommend movies, videos, or products based on your behavior and preferences.

Self-Driving Cars –

Companies like Tesla and Waymo use AI to help cars recognize objects, make decisions, and drive safely without human input.

Face Recognition –

AI is used in smartphone face unlock, security cameras, and surveillance systems to identify individuals.

Chatbots –

Many websites and apps use AI-powered chatbots for customer service, helping answer questions or guide users 24/7.
